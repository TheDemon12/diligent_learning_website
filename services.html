Services

We produce comprehensive study materials and step-by-step textbook solutions. We also support both live tutoring and asynchronous Q&A.

ACADEMIC CONTENT DEVELOPMENT

Our team is capable of providing customised textbooks to schools, universities and other academic institutions in India and abroad. The team has networked subject experts for almost all STEM subjects.

PROVIDING TEXTBOOK SOLUTIONS

We specialise in providing a detailed step by step solution manual for textbooks for which only answers are available in textbook. It guides the student and enables them to write the answer along with the solution in a structured manner.

ASSIGNMENT ASSISTANCE

Our team has recruited passionate subject experts to help the students with the assignments provided at school and/or colleges. These experts would help the students understand the assignment and will also guide them as to how to utilise their subject knowledge to do the assignment.

Founded in 2013, Diligent Learning is a leading brand in educational content development across all STEM and Business subjects. It is the result of our meticulous preparation, hard work, and years of experience delivering quality content to students globally.
